About the Project

Water scarcity and excess pose critical challenges. Our tool uses advanced AI models to forecast water availability and demand, enabling better resource management.

Project Objectives

  • Climate-Driven Forecasting: Anticipate water availability based on climate projections.
  • Water Demand Forecasting: Predict future demand based on historical and socio-demographic data.
  • Decision Support: Provide user-friendly tools for sustainable water resource management.
